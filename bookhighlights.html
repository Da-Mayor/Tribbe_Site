Book Highlights

Book Highlight [ book hahy-lahyt ]

noun

1. a condensed version of a printed work using block quotes of its most important information

2. the essence of a book without the extended exposition

“Book Highlights” was created to share my enthusiasm about new books with friends and family, who most likely, wouldn’t read the new books. I thought maybe they’ll read a condensed version. In the beginning I tried writing summaries but felt the essence of the author and crucial information was missed, so instead I block quoted what I thought was the most important information from each chapter, and voila! A 300 page dense behemoth was condensed to 15 pages of its most important parts.
